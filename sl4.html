Saint Anthony of Padua

Saint Anthony's Miracles

These are just some of his miracles.

Teleported
One night when he was preaching on Holy Thursday, he remembered that he had to sing a lesson. In the blink of an eye, he paused his preaching, and appeared in the choir to sing, after which he resumed his preaching.
Protected
When he was preaching, it started raining, but the audience never got wet.
Predicted
Saint Anthony predicted that the pulpit (the stage which you preach on) would be overthrown but nobody would get hurt; he was right. Not even the saint preaching got hurt.
